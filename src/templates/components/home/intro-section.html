Dare to cross the boundary of the known. The Zarathar archipelago stretches between sea and mist, forged by the wrath of titans, a theater of greedy colonization and centuries-old resistance. Here, the ashes of an immortal volcano nourish flamboyant jungles, draconic spirits watch in the shadows, and each island harbors its own mysteries — or nightmares.

Colonizers seeking fortune, survivors from the Old World, or mist pirates, all come to try their luck on these untamed lands. But nothing is simple in Zarathar: magic flows in stone and water, gems whisper their secrets, and alliances are made and broken under the threat of rival factions — or forgotten creatures.

Here, the boundary between history and legend crumbles with each step: a masked Council dictates its law in the city of Aquila, the Draconids defend secrets that none dare name, and the mists, capricious, sometimes open onto other worlds.

Zarathar is a world where every choice has a price. The ruins of the Primordial Battle haunt the present; the cycle of conquest and rebellion shapes the destiny of each adventurer. Will you play the pioneers, the rebels, the discoverers, or the traitors? Welcome to Zarathar: the last frontier, where only those who know how to read the signs — or shape them — survive.
